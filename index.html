About the Evening

An Evening of Community, Celebration & Purpose

The September Standard is an intimate community gala bringing together 400 friends, families, and supporters for a night of cocktails, open bar, hors d'oeuvres, and live programming at 74Wythe in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. Doors open at 7:30 PM; the main event runs 8:00–11:00 PM. We recommend eating beforehand — this is a night to celebrate, not sit.

After the formal programming wraps at 11, the party continues at a TBD location nearby for pizza, great music, and the kind of conversation that only happens when the night gets going. Details to follow.

All proceeds benefit Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center — one of the world's foremost cancer research and treatment institutions. MSK's work spans pioneering clinical trials, compassionate patient care, and scientific discovery that reaches patients around the globe. This is our community's way of holding the line for them, year after year.
